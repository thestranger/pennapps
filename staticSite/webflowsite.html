The best way of keeping track of employees!

Get rid of timecards and more accurately track when employees enter and leave work 

No more clocking in:

Your employees are automatically clocked in when they enter the workplace. No punchcards. No app logins. No unreliable facial recognition. 

Honesty that goes both ways: 

Now you know exactly how long your employees are at work. With TrackTime, employees can't clock in for their co-workers, or take long, unclocked breaks. This benefits both your company and your employees, who know that they're being fairly compensated for their work. 

Works for everybody:

With both Android and iPhone support, there's no need to get specialized devices for each employee.

How TrackTime Works

TrackTime uses a revolutionary new technology called iBeacons that allows smartphones to easily communicate with receivers

1

Beacon Box

A Beacon Box is a small device that you place on your wall near the entrance of your business. It broadcasts a special signal that is used for tracking workers.

2

Employee's Phone

When an employee comes into work, his phone automatically receives the signal and signs him. When he leaves the office, the phone automatically logs out.

3

Portal

Using our unique portal, you can see the status of  your employees, including whether they are at work, what time they arrived, and how long they worked
